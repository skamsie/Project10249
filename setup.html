Recording Setup

This is the setup I used to record videos using music directly from vinyl. The video was recorded with the laptop's FaceTime HD Camera (Built-in) and the sound directly from viny lusing the M Audio sound card. All the processing was made with iMovie.

Equipment used:

  1. Technics 1200 / Numark TT500
  2. Denon Dn-X120
  3. Macbook Pro (mid 2012)
  4. M Audio Firewire
  5. QuickTime Player 7 (software)
  6. KRK Rokit 5 (for monitoring)
